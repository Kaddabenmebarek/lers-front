Latest news


Manage reservation with priority users (15 May 2023)
It is now possible to set priority users for an instrument. Other users will be able to book the instrument only for a short period that can defined as retention days.
Add your own instrument (15 May 2023)
Instead of requesting for a new instrument with a ticket on Assist, you can now register your own instrument.
Highlight instruction for a reservation (15 May 2023)
When editing an instrument, you can now make the instructions you give for the reservation highlighted.
Manage reservation ratio (01 Dec 2022)
Owners and deputies can now manage instrument to set multi reservation and ratio step for a given instrument.
New Widget (10 June 2022)
A new button with shortcuts to research web applications has been added.
Synthesis Planner (09 June 2022)
A new planner is available for Synthesis group to show the scheduled orders.
The requester will be informed by email when the order will be ready and withdrawable.
HTMC Planner (09 June 2022)
A new planner is available for HTMC group to show the scheduled Libraries.
The requester will also be informed by email when the order will be ready.
Reports (09 June 2022)
Reports for the Instruments reservations, Synthesis planner and HTMC planner can be generated.
